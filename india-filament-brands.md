## Why This Matters for Indian Printers

- India's **humid climate** (especially coastal cities and monsoon season) accelerates moisture absorption — vacuum-sealed spools with desiccant packs are essential
- **Import duties and shipping** make international brands 40–80% more expensive than their global price
- **After-sales support** from Indian brands is significantly faster for replacements or quality issues
- All brands below are **1.75 mm diameter** and compatible with the BambuLab P1S (no AMS RFID tag unless noted)

## P1S-Specific Notes

- **No brand** other than Bambu Lab has RFID tags — all third-party filaments require manual profile selection
- For **AMS use**: eSUN, NuMakers, and Polymaker all feed reliably. SUNLU has occasional tangle reports. FibReel not widely tested with AMS
- **Humidity warning**: India's climate is harsh on open filaments. All brands — including Indian ones — should be stored in sealed containers with silica desiccant. PETG, ABS, ASA, and Nylon absorb moisture within hours in monsoon conditions
- **Cardboard spools** (Polymaker PolyTerra): Store in a zip-lock bag inside a sealed box — the spool itself can swell and jam
